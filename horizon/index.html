About Us

We Are

Horizon Flight Academy stands at the forefront of aviation education, dedicated to training the next generation of skilled and confident pilots. Our mission is to provide comprehensive, high-quality training that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and experience needed to excel in the aviation industry. Whether you're aiming to earn your private pilot license or pursuing advanced certifications, Horizon Flight Academy offers a range of programs designed to meet diverse goals and aspirations.

Our academy features a state-of-the-art fleet, including single and multi-engine aircraft, equipped with the latest avionics and safety features to ensure a modern and realistic training experience. Our team of highly qualified instructors brings years of expertise, providing personalized instruction that adapts to each student's pace and learning style. We are committed to creating a supportive and motivating environment where students feel confident and empowered to achieve their best.
